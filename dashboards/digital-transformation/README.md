# Digital Transformation Dashboard
## لوحة التحول الرقمي

**Module:** DASH-08
**Phase:** 4
**Status:** Planned

---

## Purpose

Tracks the progress, adoption, and ROI of all digital transformation initiatives across the organization — providing executive leadership with a clear picture of the digital transformation journey.

---

## Key Widgets (Planned)

| Widget | Data Source | Refresh |
|---|---|---|
| Initiative tracker (status, owner, % complete) | Internal / ERPNext | Weekly |
| Digital adoption metrics per system | System APIs | Weekly |
| Digital transaction % (vs. manual) | ERPNext + Systems | Monthly |
| Transformation roadmap progress | Internal | Weekly |
| ROI tracker for completed initiatives | Finance + Internal | Monthly |
| Technology dependency map (visual) | Internal | Quarterly |
| Change management readiness indicator | Internal / Survey | Monthly |
